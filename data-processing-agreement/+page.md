# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Effective Date: November 6, 2025
Last Updated: November 6, 2025

This Data Processing Agreement (“DPA”) forms part of the Palaba Terms of Service (“Agreement”) between the user (“You”) and Parsonos Corporation (“Palaba,” “we,” “us,” “our”).
This DPA applies only to personal, non-enterprise use of the Palaba platform.
In the English-language market worldwide: For enterprise publishing, multi-seat use, or commercial audiobook production, a separate enterprise agreement is required through Rhapid Software Solutions Inc. Contact: support@rhapidsoftware.com

## 1. Roles
- You are the Data Controller of personal data you upload.
- Palaba acts as the Data Processor in processing that data solely to provide the Service.

We do not decide the purposes of your processing. You do.

## 2. Purpose of Processing

We process User Content and personal data only to:
- Provide script-aligned audio assembly
- Maintain, secure and improve the Service
- Facilitate user access and account management

We do not:
- Train **generative** AI models on your content
- Sell or share your data
- Use your content for marketing

## 3. Categories of Data

We may process the following types of data:
- Uploaded recordings and scripts
- Account information (name, email, login details)
- Usage metrics (tool functions used, processing time, logs)
- Optional project notes/tags

**We do not request or knowingly process sensitive personal data. You agree not to upload health data, biometric identifiers, or comparable sensitive information.**

## 4. Instructions

We process data only according to your documented instructions, which include:
- Creating and managing your projects
- Generating session outputs
- Deleting content at your request

If we cannot comply with instructions, we will notify you.

## 5. Subprocessors

We may use vetted subprocessors (e.g., cloud infrastructure) to operate the Service.
A list of subprocessors is available on request. We ensure each subprocessor provides GDPR- and CCPA-level protection.

## 6. International Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States or other jurisdictions with adequate safeguards.
Where applicable, we rely on:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), or
- Other lawful transfer mechanisms

## 7. Security

We maintain reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational measures, including:
- Encrypted data storage and transit
- Access controls
- Audit logs
- Vulnerability management

## 8. Data Subject Rights
If you are in the EU/EEA, UK, or California, you may request:
- Access to data
- Correction
- Deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
- Data portability
- Restriction or objection to processing

Contact: legal@parsonos.com 

We will respond within required legal timelines.

## 9. Data Retention & Deletion

- You may delete content at any time
- We delete associated processing data shortly after
- Anonymous usage metrics may be retained to support system reliability

We honor deletion requests unless retention is required by law.

## 10. Breach Notification

If a data breach affecting your personal data occurs, we will notify you without undue delay and consistent with applicable law.

## 11. Limitation of Use

This DPA does not apply to:
- Enterprise or publisher use
- Studio/vendor pipelines
- High-volume professional workflows
Those require a separate Enterprise DPA through Rhapid.

## 12. Term & Termination

This DPA remains in effect as long as we process your data. Upon termination of your account, data will be deleted in accordance with this DPA.

## 13. Priority

If this DPA conflicts with the ToS, this DPA controls only for privacy and data processing matters.

## 14. Contact

Data protection inquiries: legal@parsonos.com
